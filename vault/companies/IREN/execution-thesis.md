## Caveats / Methodology

- **This is execution synthesis, not prediction.** Confidence describes evidence quality, not outcome probability.
- **Stale-source bias.** All Tier-C signals here are within 30 days of refresh date.
- **EDGAR ≠ Tier A.** Press releases, decks, and earnings-call transcripts furnished through EDGAR are still company outputs. They are Tier B per the skill's strict tier rule. Use the Form 10-K / 10-Q / 8-K item disclosure for the same fact where available.
- **Milestone separation.** Substation energization, data-center construction, customer delivery acceptance, and revenue ramp are four different milestones. Conflating them creates false "slip" findings — H3 softened accordingly.
- **The $11.9B commitments cliff reframes near-term execution risk.** Pre-this-refresh, the narrative was "buildout on schedule, dilution drumbeat in background." Post-10-Q-extraction: **the funding-vs-commitments race IS the dominant near-term execution variable.** H1 confidence remains HIGH because the funding stack is keeping pace, but the margin of safety is much thinner than the headline "$2.2B cash + $1.94B Microsoft prepay + $3.6B Goldman/JPM commitment" frame implied. A material miss on the May 11 convertibles pricing OR the Goldman/JPM definitive agreement would crack H1 fast.
- **Management-statement reliability.** IREN mgmt has high track record on stated execution gates (Sweetwater 1 energization on the targeted date; Microsoft contract delivered as announced; NVIDIA contract layered on top). Mgmt-disclosed slowing of "remaining" capacity ramp to 2028 is itself a credibility-positive disclosure act.
- **H2 confabulation risk applies particularly to the Project Iris Monarch claim.** The fan-site source is Tier-C; the alleged AEP source is Tier-D; the alleged county records would be Tier-A IF verified. Until primary verification, this is a CANDIDATE, not evidence. The 60-day verification action is the discipline that protects the gate.
- **Pattern-matching on IE US Development Holdings entities is Tier-D inference.** Holdings 3 is verified via Texas Comptroller + SEC 10-K Exhibit 21.1. Holdings 5 and 7 are NOT in the 10-K Exhibit 21.1 significant-subsidiaries list — they may be tracker artifacts, not legal entities.
- **Founder Form 4 silence since Sep 11, 2025** is a governance flag (positive direction this time). Belongs in deep-dive's Founder Signal section, not this file's hypotheses.
