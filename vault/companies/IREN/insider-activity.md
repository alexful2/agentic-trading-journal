# IREN — Insider Activity

Sourced from Form 3, Form 4, and Form 144 filings on EDGAR (CIK 0001878848). IREN's Section 16 reporting started **mid-2025** because IREN lost foreign private issuer status as of December 31, 2024. Prior to that, insider transactions were not reported via Form 4 — they were disclosed in 20-F annual reports only. So the "insider activity timeline" effectively begins July 2025.

This means **historical insider sale patterns under the FPI regime are NOT captured here** — IREN had been public since November 2021, and 4 years of insider activity have no Form 4 record. Form 144 filings exist back to 2023, including a December 2023 cluster (Mr. Bartholomew or affiliated entities, by accession pattern).

## 10b5-1 plan analysis

**Key signal: the September 2025 Roberts brothers' transactions were NOT under 10b5-1 plans.**

In each of the four September Form 4 filings (D Roberts 9/5 + 9/11, W Roberts 9/5 + 9/11), the `<aff10b5One>` element is set to `0`, meaning the trade was **not made pursuant to a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan**. These are "opportunistic" sales — discretionary timing decisions.

Caveats on signal interpretation:
1. **The 9/3-9/4 option transactions were time-forced.** The pre-IPO options expire Dec 20, 2025. With ~3 months left to expiration, exercising and selling is nearly mandatory to monetize. Treating these as bearish-conviction signal would over-weight a forced action.
2. **The 9/11 ordinary share sale is more discretionary.** $33M each (66M total Roberts brothers' liquidation) at $33.13 — IREN's stock subsequently moved higher, reaching $41.12 by the Dec 2, 2025 capital raise reference price. The pricing context: these are not founders cashing out at a peak (stock kept moving up), but they are taking the first meaningful liquidity since their July 2025 Section 16 filing-eligibility began.
3. **No 10b5-1 plan adoption notice for the Roberts brothers** has been filed via Form 4 disclosure or dedicated filing as of the dossier refresh date. This is unusual for newly-Section-16-filing co-CEOs — typically founders adopt 10b5-1 plans early to enable systematic disposition without trading-window restrictions.

## Signals to watch

When the next Form 4 batch arrives, the relevant calibration points are:

1. **Did the Roberts brothers adopt 10b5-1 plans in late 2025 or early 2026?** If yes → ongoing sales would be programmed and lower-signal. If no → continued opportunistic discretionary sales remain a higher-signal data point.
2. **Were there sales around the Dec 8, 2025 capital raise** ($41.12 reference price)? Any Form 4 dated Dec 2025 or shortly after would be material — particularly if at or above $41 and not 10b5-1.
3. **CFO transition behavior.** Anthony Lewis became CFO Sep 8, 2025 with prior role as Chief Capital Officer. His Form 3 establishes baseline holdings. Material grants or sales by Lewis in FY26 are a fresh datapoint not yet in the record.
4. **Any disposition by independent directors.** As of the dossier refresh date, none of the four independent directors have filed disposition Form 4s. Sales by those holders would be a different signal type than founder sales (typically lower trading-volume but a closer read of "informed insiders without 100% conviction").
5. **Microsoft contract execution and Form 4 timing.** The $9.7B Microsoft Agreement was signed Nov 2, 2025. Tracking insider transactions around major contract milestones (announcement, first delivery, customer prepayment receipt) is informative.

## Calibration framing for `news-analyst`

When daily news items cover insider transactions for IREN:

- **Pre-IPO option exercises near expiration (e.g., Dec 2025 options):** Largely mechanical. Sev 1-2 unless the volume is order-of-magnitude larger than expected.
- **Ordinary share sales NOT under 10b5-1:** Higher-signal. Sev 3 baseline; sev 4 if sales are large (>0.5% of pre-tx insider stake) or coincident with material disclosure events.
- **Adoption of 10b5-1 plan:** Lower-signal for subsequent sales; **but the adoption itself** can be sev 2-3 if it follows a pattern of opportunistic selling and signals systematic liquidation intent.
- **Director sales (independent directors):** Sev 2-3 — these are typically diversification rather than informed selling.
- **Founder sales above prior price levels (e.g., above $41 reference price):** Watch for sev-3 framing with explicit comparison to the Sep 2025 baseline of $33.13.

## Sample size limitations

This dossier reflects only the Form 4 / Form 3 / Form 144 record visible since Section 16 reporting began (July 2025). Earlier sales by the Roberts brothers and others under the FPI 20-F regime are not captured. **Avoid characterizing the Roberts brothers' selling as "first-ever sales" or as establishing a pattern** — there may be undocumented sales in 2022-2024 that are simply not in EDGAR's Section 16 system.

The Form 144 trail back to 2023 hints at insider sales activity during the FPI period; the specific amounts and identities can be parsed from the individual 144 primary docs if needed for a deeper insider history review.
