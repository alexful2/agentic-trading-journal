**Dec 8, 2025 capital raise summary:**
- $2.30B aggregate convertible notes (2032 + 2033)
- $1.63B equity (39,699,102 ordinary shares @ $41.12 = $1,632.4M gross)
- $0.20B used to fund Dec capped call transactions
- $1.63B used to repurchase 2029 + 2030 convertibles (above market — $544.3M principal repurchased for $1,632.4M = ~3× principal cost)
- ~$2.07B remaining for general corporate purposes / working capital

10-K FY25 reported $990M of principal convertibles outstanding (the 2029+2030 originals before partial buyback). Post-Dec 2025 the structure is dominated by 2031/2032/2033 paper at lower coupons but more share count exposure (lower conv. prices on 2032/2033 = more dilution risk if shares run).

**At-the-market (ATM) facility:** Through Dec 31, 2025, IREN had issued **66,707,732 shares** under the Sales Agreement (B. Riley/Cantor/Compass Point/Canaccord/Citi/JPM/Macquarie) at varying prices, generating **~$1.0B in proceeds**. As of that date no further amounts remained available under the current prospectus supplement; IREN said it plans to register additional shares for sale potentially in the near term. (Source: 10-Q Q2 FY26)

**Equipment financing:**
- **NYDIG legacy:** Two non-recourse SPV equipment financing facilities defaulted Nov 2022 (~$107.8M asserted indebtedness). Settlement agreement signed Aug 12, 2025 — IREN to pay settlement amount, of which $18.2M exceeds prior accruals. Closure pending court approval; Canadian bankruptcy proceedings expected to take ~6 months to finalize.
- **Dell Financial Services:** Nov 5, 2025 — $199.8M financing structured as 24-month lease with $1 purchase option; IREN parent-guaranteed.
- **Dell direct purchase:** Mar 4, 2026 — $2.3B (Canada) + $1.2B (USA) = $3.5B GPU purchase agreements, paid in installments within 30 days of each tranche shipping (2H 2026 delivery), IREN parent-guaranteed. Note this is a direct purchase, not lease; financing structure for these payments not yet disclosed.

## Customer revenue concentration

- **Bitcoin mining:** Revenue earned from Antpool and Foundry mining pools (statistically expected pro-rata share of network rewards). Mathematically diversified across the Bitcoin network — no single customer concentration.
- **AI Cloud Services:** Explicit risk-factor disclosure that revenue is concentrated in a small number of customers, many of whom are early-stage / private AI companies. As of Q2 FY26, the **Microsoft Agreement** ($9.7B total contract value, 5-year average term, 20% prepay per tranche) is now the dominant disclosed AI customer commitment. As of Dec 31, 2025 no Microsoft tranches had been delivered/accepted, so $0 in unsatisfied RPO recognized yet.

## Liquidity guidance

In the Q2 FY26 10-Q (filed Feb 5, 2026), management stated existing cash + expected operating cash flow + future financings will be sufficient for the next 12 months and "reasonably foreseeable future." Capital requirements call out the Microsoft Agreement and Horizon 1/2/3/4 build-out as the largest near-term funding obligations. Management is actively exploring "alternative financings" — language consistent with continued ATM sales and possibly more convertible issuance. (Source: 10-Q Q2 FY26 Liquidity and Capital Resources)

## Tax & GST contingencies

- **Canada GST/HST audit:** Subsidiaries currently subject to CRA audit and an administrative appeal on input tax credits. Could reduce historical and going-forward credits.
- **CRA permanent-establishment dispute:** June 23, 2025 — IREN filed Notice of Appeal with Tax Court of Canada disputing CRA determination that IREN has a Canadian PE and the related GST assessment.
- **US Customs tariff dispute:** Apr 2025 NOA disputing country-of-origin on Bitcoin miners imported Apr 2024-Feb 2025 — potential additional tariffs **up to ~$100M** if challenge fails.

## Securities class action

Pending putative securities class action in US District Court NJ. Originally filed Dec 14, 2022; second amended complaint filed Nov 12, 2024 alleging Section 10(b)/20(a) and Section 11/12(a)(2)/15 claims tied to IPO Registration Statement and statements between Nov 17, 2021 and Nov 1, 2022. Motion to dismiss fully briefed; pending. IREN intends to defend vigorously. (Source: 10-K FY25 Item 3, Note 7)
