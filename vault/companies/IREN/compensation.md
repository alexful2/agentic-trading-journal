# IREN — Executive Compensation

Sourced from DEF 14A filed 2025-10-06 (covering Fiscal 2025 = year ended June 30, 2025). Compensation consultant: FW Cook.

## Compensation philosophy (CD&A)

- **Pay for performance:** 93% of target annual pay for Co-CEOs and 66% for the (then-)CFO is variable/at-risk for FY25.
- At-risk comp includes short-term annual cash bonuses + time-based RSUs (TRSUs) + performance-based RSUs (PRSUs).
- PRSUs are share-price-performance dependent; TRSUs vest on time-based schedule.
- Belinda Nucifora's compensation paid in AUD; converted to USD using 12-month average rate of 0.6454 USD/AUD for FY25.

## Compensation observations

1. **The FY25 stock awards spike is huge.** $69.7M each for the Co-CEOs is ~10× their prior-year stock comp. Even allowing for performance-vesting on PRSUs, this represents a step-change in equity grant scale.
2. **Salaries declined materially** from FY23 ($2.4M) → FY24 ($1.6M) → FY25 ($964k). Cash comp is being shifted to equity, consistent with the at-risk philosophy stated in CD&A.
3. **STI paid at maximum** in FY25 ($1.968M each, not the $984k target).
4. **Symmetry:** Co-CEO comp is identical at every line item every year — no individual differentiation in pay.
5. **Stock-based compensation expense was $42.6M in FY25** (per cash flow statement) but **$130.6M in just H1 FY26** — indicating significantly heavier equity grants are flowing through P&L in the current fiscal year, well above the FY25 run rate. Selectively read FY26 DEF 14A when filed (likely late summer/fall 2026) for the new comp picture.
