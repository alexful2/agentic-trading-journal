## Key facts agent must know before recommending

- **IREN converted from foreign private issuer (FPI) to domestic filer effective Dec 31, 2024.** First 10-K filed Aug 28, 2025; first 10-Q (Q1 FY26) filed Nov 6, 2025; Q2 FY26 10-Q filed Feb 5, 2026. Section 16 reporting (Form 3/4/5) started July 2025. Insider sale history under FPI regime (2021-2024) is NOT in Form 4 record.
- **Australian-incorporated with dual-class voting.** Two B Class shares held by Roberts brothers carry 43.5% combined voting power on a 4.6% economic stake. B shares mechanically redeemed Nov 17, 2033 absent earlier trigger.
- **Fiscal year ends June 30.** FY2025 = year ended June 30, 2025. Q1 FY26 ended Sep 30, 2025; Q2 FY26 ended Dec 31, 2025; Q3 FY26 (most recent expected next print) ends Mar 31, 2026.
- **Microsoft Agreement (Nov 2, 2025) is the marquee customer commitment** — $9.7B over ~5 years, 20% prepay per tranche, $0 of unsatisfied RPO recognized as of Dec 31, 2025 because no tranches delivered yet. Watch for first revenue recognition disclosure in upcoming 10-Qs.
- **Dec 8, 2025 mega capital raise:** $2.3B convertibles (2032 + 2033 @ $51.40 conversion) + $1.63B equity (39.7M shares @ $41.12) + $0.20B used for capped calls. Preceded by $1.0B 2031 convertible Oct 14, 2025. Cash balance Dec 31, 2025: **$3.26B**.
- **Mar 4, 2026 Dell GPU agreements:** $3.5B aggregate ($2.3B Canada + $1.2B USA) for 2H 2026 delivery, IREN parent-guaranteed.
- **GPUs installed or on order (Dec 31, 2025):** ~99,900 (vs. ~1,900 at June 30, 2025).
- **Roberts brothers' Sep 2025 sales were NOT under 10b5-1 plans** (`aff10b5One` = 0). Each sold 1M ordinary shares @ $33.13 = $33.1M cash + monetized 1M expiring options (strike $3.27 USD, expire Dec 20, 2025). No 10b5-1 plan adoption disclosed as of Apr 2026.
- **Stock-based compensation $130.6M in H1 FY26** (vs $42.6M FY25 full year) — running at ~30% of revenue. Big step-up in equity grants flowing through P&L.
- **First profitable fiscal year was FY25** (net income $86.9M) but heavily flattered by $77.5M unrealized gain on derivative instruments. H1 FY26 net income $229.2M is similarly flattered by $557.6M unrealized derivative gain — operating income H1 FY26 was negative $193M.
- **Bitcoin mining is paused at 50 EH/s** (announced June 2025). Future hashrate growth is contingent on AI/HPC alternatives not being more attractive.
- **Pending litigation overhangs:** (1) NYDIG settlement signed Aug 12, 2025 — not yet effective until court approvals (~6 months); $18.2M payment exceeds prior accruals. (2) NJ securities class action — motion to dismiss fully briefed and pending. (3) Canada CRA permanent-establishment / GST appeal in Tax Court of Canada (filed June 23, 2025). (4) US Customs tariff dispute up to ~$100M.

- **Annual general meeting was held Nov 19, 2025** (per PRE 14A / DEF 14A). Constitutional amendments voted (quorum alignment, classified board for Non-Founder Directors, forum selection, advance notice, miscellaneous).
