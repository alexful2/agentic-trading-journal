## Co-Chief Executive Officer structure

IREN runs a **two-CEO model** — Daniel Roberts and William Roberts both hold Co-Chief Executive Officer titles. Their compensation is identical at every layer of the package (see `compensation.md`). Day-to-day operational division of labor is not explicitly documented in SEC filings.

## Governance characteristics

- **Australian-incorporated public company** with US listing on Nasdaq. Subject to Australian Corporations Act + Nasdaq listing rules.
- **Dual-class voting structure:** 2 B Class shares (held one each by Awassi Capital Trust #1 (William) and Awassi Capital Trust #2 (Daniel)) carry voting rights disproportionate to economic ownership. As of Aug 31, 2025: founders' beneficial ownership of ordinary shares is ~4.6% economic but **43.5% of total voting power**. The B Class shares are required to be redeemed on the earlier of (i) the holder ceasing to be a director due to voluntary retirement, (ii) breach-of-constitution transfer, (iii) liquidation, or (iv) **November 17, 2033** (12 years after IPO).
- **Board not currently subject to term limits** — directors hold office until succession or removal. The Director Election Amendment in the 2025 PRE 14A would change this for Non-Founder Directors only.
- **No standing nominating committee** — independent directors collectively recommend nominees.
- **Compensation Committee** uses **FW Cook** as independent compensation consultant.
- **Board met 7 times in FY25.** Audit & Risk Committee met 6 times. Compensation Committee met 3 times.
- **No formal director attendance policy at AGM**, though encouraged. 5 of 6 directors attended virtual 2024 AGM.
- **Related party transactions policy** in place; no related-party transactions exceeding $120,000 reported between July 1, 2024 and DEF 14A filing date (October 2025) outside compensation arrangements.
- **2024 AGM venue:** Virtual. **2025 AGM (the one being voted on in this DEF 14A):** Held November 19, 2025.

## Alignment signals

**Pro-alignment:**
- Founders still in place 7+ years after founding, both as Co-CEOs and on board
- 93% of co-CEO target annual pay is variable / at-risk (per CD&A)
- Independent directors hold meaningful equity (Bartholomew, Alfred, Parasuraman all have non-trivial holdings; see `ownership.md`)
- Independent compensation consultant (FW Cook)
- Audit & Risk Committee Chair is a designated audit committee financial expert (Parasuraman)

**Anti-alignment:**
- Dual-class B-share voting concentrates control with founders out of proportion to economic stake
- Classified board proposal in 2025 DEF 14A would limit shareholder ability to change board composition for several years
- Founder Directors are exempt from the proposed annual-election regime
- Roberts brothers sold ~$33M each of ordinary shares + ~$22M each of options in early Sep 2025 (NOT under 10b5-1 plan — see `insider-activity.md`). Sales were partly forced by Dec 20, 2025 expiration of pre-IPO options, but Sept 11 ordinary share sale was discretionary

**Family relationship to flag:** Daniel and William Roberts are brothers. Both serve as directors and Co-CEOs. This is unusual at the Co-CEO level for a publicly traded company and creates concentrated family control distinct from typical founder-CEO setups.
